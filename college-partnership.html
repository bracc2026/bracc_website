College Partnership

Collaborating with colleges to create awareness about modern global career opportunities in US Taxation and Accounting.

Why College Collaboration Matters

Many commerce and finance students are unaware of emerging global career opportunities available in US Taxation, outsourcing, and international accounting industries.

Academic curriculums often focus primarily on theoretical learning, while industry expectations increasingly demand practical exposure, global awareness, and specialized skillsets.

BRACC aims to support colleges by conducting awareness-oriented sessions, career guidance initiatives, and practical industry discussions for students.

Possible Areas of Collaboration

🎓 Career Awareness Sessions

Interactive sessions introducing students to global accounting and US Taxation opportunities.

📘 EA Qualification Awareness

Helping students understand globally recognized credentials such as EA and related career pathways.

💼 Industry Insights

Exposure to outsourcing industries, Big 4 environments, and modern accounting career expectations.

🚀 Student Guidance Initiatives

Mentorship-oriented discussions focused on career clarity, skill awareness, and future opportunities.

BRACC Vision

BRACC believes students benefit significantly when academic learning is supported with practical industry awareness and global career exposure.

The initiative focuses on helping students understand modern accounting career landscapes through mentorship, discussions, webinars, and structured guidance efforts.

BRACC aims to collaborate with colleges interested in creating additional industry awareness and future career exposure for their students.
