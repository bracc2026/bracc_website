Build a Global Career in US Taxation with the Enrolled Agent (EA) Qualification

What is EA?

Enrolled Agent (EA) is a globally recognized US taxation credential authorized by the Internal Revenue Service (IRS), USA.

EA professionals specialize in US taxation, tax representation, compliance, advisory, and global tax processes.

The qualification is highly valued by Big 4 firms, outsourcing companies, consulting firms, and multinational organizations involved in US taxation services.

Why Choose EA?

🌍 Global Recognition

Recognized internationally in US Taxation and accounting industries.

💼 Strong Career Opportunities

Opportunities in Big 4 firms, outsourcing companies, and global consulting firms.

📈 Career Growth

Build long-term careers in US Taxation, advisory, compliance, and consulting.

🎯 Specialized Skillset

Gain expertise in one of the fastest growing global accounting domains.

How BRACC Helps

BRACC focuses on practical guidance, mentorship, career awareness, and industry-oriented learning for students and professionals interested in US Taxation careers.

Our goal is to simplify the EA journey and help candidates understand the practical industry expectations beyond theoretical concepts.

Through webinars, guidance sessions, and structured learning initiatives, BRACC aims to support aspiring professionals in building industry-ready careers.
