About BRACC

BRACC is a professional mentoring and learning initiative focused on helping students and working professionals build careers in US Taxation and Accounting with globally recognized credentials such as EA.

Our vision is to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world industry expectations through practical training, structured guidance, mentorship, and career-oriented learning.

BRACC aims to create industry-ready professionals by combining technical knowledge with practical exposure to global accounting and taxation opportunities.

BRACC is focused on guiding commerce and finance students and professionals towards career opportunities in US Taxation, EA, and global accounting industries through practical industry-oriented learning.
