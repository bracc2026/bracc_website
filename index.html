BRACC
(Beyond Reach for Accounting & Career Credentials)


BRACC is a professional mentoring and learning initiative focused on helping aspiring and working professionals build strong careers in US Taxation, Accounting and global professional credentials such as EA and CPA.

The vision behind BRACC is to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world industry expectations through practical guidance, structured learning and mentorship-oriented support.

BRACC is focused on guiding commerce and finance students and professionals towards career opportunities in US Taxation, EA, and global accounting industries through practical industry-oriented learning.

What We Offer

Career Guidance

Understand opportunities in US Taxation and global accounting industries.

EA Awareness

Learn about the Enrolled Agent qualification and career pathways.

Practical Learning

Gain industry-oriented exposure beyond theoretical concepts.

Industry Insights

Learn how outsourcing firms operate and the skills they expect.
