About BRACC

Building awareness, guidance, and practical career direction in US Taxation & Accounting.

Who We Are

BRACC is a professional mentoring and learning initiative focused on helping students and working professionals better understand career opportunities in US Taxation, Accounting, and global professional domains.

The initiative aims to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world industry expectations through practical awareness, mentorship-oriented guidance, and career-focused learning discussions.

BRACC focuses on creating industry awareness around global accounting environments, outsourcing industries, EA qualification pathways, and evolving opportunities within US Taxation services.

What BRACC Focuses On

🎯 Career Awareness

Helping students and professionals understand modern global accounting and taxation career pathways.

📘 Practical Learning

Encouraging industry-oriented understanding beyond purely theoretical education.

💼 Industry Exposure

Providing insights into outsourcing industries, Big 4 environments, and global accounting expectations.

🚀 Mentorship & Guidance

Supporting aspiring professionals through structured discussions, webinars, and learning initiatives.

BRACC Vision

BRACC believes future accounting professionals benefit significantly when academic knowledge is combined with practical industry awareness and global career exposure.

The initiative is designed to help aspiring professionals gain clarity, confidence, and direction regarding modern accounting and US Taxation career opportunities.

Through awareness sessions, webinars, mentorship initiatives, and future learning programs, BRACC aims to support the development of industry-ready professionals.
